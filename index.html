Audio Reconstruction Comparison

We analyze the spectral reconstruction capabilities of our model and compare with an ablated version (SLOW) without a fast generator learning to reconstruct the full spectrogram, and RAVE trained on the Representation Learning objective. We show reconstructed excerpts from the test set.
For reference, we also add RAVE, trained with an adversarial step. Although it shown worse spectral reconstruction results, we believe it presents better audio quality. We leave for future work an exploration of adversarial training possibilities in a latency-hiding configuration.
